At the end of our programme, we believe students will better understand their process of thinking, and appreciate how that understanding can be used to think incisively in different situations. This will equip students to engage with argumentation faster and more accurately, making them more comfortable with both explaining themselves and responding to the views of others.

This has direct implications for studentsâ€™ academic life. In the short term, this translates to greater efficiency in reading to identify the specific answers for questions, and clearer written responses; this is of particular use when faced with unfamiliar questions, in examinations or otherwise. In the long term, we think this will help students adapt to the challenging intellectual environment of higher education where such thinking is demanded as a matter of course.
